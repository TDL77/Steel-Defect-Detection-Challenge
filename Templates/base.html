Steel Defect Detection

We see that steel is ubiquitous in nature. It is present in numerous industries such as buildings, mechanical equipments, automotive, metal, and transport industries.

Looking at a lot of applications of steel, manufacturing of steel is important so that it matches the demand for various industries.

There are, however, defects in steel that arise due to extremely cold temperatures when pouring the steel mould when it's not hot enough and so on. Sometimes, certain parts of steel might cool much quicker than the others causing hot spot defects. There are many other problems associated when manufacturing and using steel.

Therefore, proper steps must be taken to address this issue of defects in steel.
