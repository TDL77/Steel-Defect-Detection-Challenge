Steel Defect Detection

We see that steel is ubiquitous in nature. It is present in numerous industries such as buildings, mechanical equipments, automotive, metal, and transport industries.

Looking at a lot of applications of steel, manufacturing of steel is important so that it matches the demand for various industries.

There are, however, defects in steel that arise due to extremely cold temperatures when pouring the steel mould when it's not hot enough and so on. Sometimes, certain parts of steel might cool much quicker than the others causing hot spot defects. There are many other problems associated when manufacturing and using steel.

Therefore, proper steps must be taken to address this issue of defects in steel. One way would be to properly identify the defects manually by employing a lot of human labor in the process. Nonetheless, this process is time-confusing and often difficult. Therefore, alternate steps must be taken to improve the productivity and efficiency of producing steel respectively.

Convoluted Neural Networks

Convoluted Neural Network work extremely well with image data. They are able to accurately classify the image depending the pre-trained networks used. The process used when creating this network is called "transfer learning". The weights of the networks that were already trained in the previous tasks are taken. Only the last few layers are trained for our task of detecting the defects in steel. Therefore, it would give us a very good accuracy as the pre-trained models performed well on the task that they were assigned.
